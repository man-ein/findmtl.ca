Last updated: December 31, 2025

At findmtl.ca, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or use our services, including SMS/text messaging programs.

2. SMS / Text Message Marketing

When you opt-in to receive SMS messages from findmtl.ca, you agree to receive recurring marketing and transactional text messages to the mobile number you provided.

Message frequency varies. Message & data rates may apply. Simply text STOP to cancel at any time. Text HELP for assistance.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve our services and tools
  • Send promotional SMS messages (only if you opt-in)
  • Respond to inquiries and provide support

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We only share data with trusted service providers (like SMS platforms) who assist in our operations or when required by law.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information and opt-out of marketing communications at any time.
