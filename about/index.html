About FindMTL.ca

Your Comprehensive Guide to Montreal's Jewish Community

FindMTL.ca is a dedicated resource created to serve Montreal's vibrant Jewish community by providing easy access to essential information and connections.

We aim to be your one-stop directory for everything from shuls and minyanim to kosher food, grocery stores, restaurants, schools, simcha halls, daycares, organizations, mikvehs, events, shiurim, summer camps, eruv status, and more.

Whether you're a longtime resident, new to the city, or just visiting, FindMTL.ca helps you find what you need quickly and reliably.

We're constantly updating and expanding our listings to better serve the community. If you have suggestions or would like to add a listing, please get in touch!
